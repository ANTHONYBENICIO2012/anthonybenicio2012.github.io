# 🌟 LitWithParticles - Unique 2D Lighting Made Simple

## 🚀 Getting Started

Welcome to LitWithParticles! This application provides a unique 2D texel lighting system. Unlike typical systems, it does not use rays to evaluate brightness. Instead, it uses particles emitted from the light source to render brightness more dynamically. This method adds depth and appeal to your graphics, making your projects stand out.

## 🎨 How to Use

After installing LitWithParticles, follow these steps to create your own lighting effects:

1. **Open the Application**: Locate and click on the LitWithParticles icon to launch.
2. **Import Your Project**: Load the 2D project where you want to apply the lighting effects.
3. **Add Light Sources**: Select light sources from the toolbar. You can adjust the properties like intensity and color.
4. **Adjust Particle Settings**: Use the particle settings panel to change the emission rate, size, and spread of light.
5. **Render Your Scene**: Once you’re satisfied with your settings, click the render button to view your graphics with the new lighting effects.
